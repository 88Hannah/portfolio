About me

Update June 2024: This portfolio hasn't been updated for a little while. Please feel free to look around, and also visit my GitHub account and LinkedIn profile to see my most recent projects and activities.

Everybody will remember the year 2020. For a few years I had been planning for 2020 to be a big year in my life. I’d been saving up and finally had enough money and time to go travelling. A group of us set off from the UK in early January and didn’t expect to return until Christmas. We were having a great time exploring new parts of the world and we were just starting to get used to our new life on the road when the travel restrictions due to COVID-19 came into force and we reluctantly headed back to the UK nine months earlier than planned.

For the past three years I’ve thought about becoming a web developer. With my mathematical background and creative mind it seemed like a good fit but life got in the way and it just never seemed to quite work out. Instead, I’ve spent the last two and a half years working as a business analyst and software tester. I played a part in nearly all areas of the software development life cycle except the one that I wanted. Working closely with the developers made me realise just how much I wanted to do what they were doing.

So, when in April 2020 I found myself unexpectedly back home with no firm plans, I decided that the lockdown was the perfect opportunity for me to learn to code. I got stuck in, completed numerous online courses and I’m currently enjoying putting what I’ve learnt into practice. Have a look at my portfolio to see the projects that I have been working on.
